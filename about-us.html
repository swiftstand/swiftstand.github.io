How does the bridge work ?

For THE-BRIDGE to work in full capability we freely on-board your department by connecting with your department and getting some informations like your name and email and then we onboard the students and staffs of the department.

With this full onboardment of the department students and staffs, infomations can be passed around easily and direct informations can be easily passed to a student that needs to hear it.

Can i register alone as a single person ?

Yes you can register as single user but this limits the experience you get to only tach skill acquisition till when your department is fully onboard.

If you will like to onboard your department just speak with us and leave the convincing for us to handle and we will have your department onboard in no time.

What are needed to onboard a department ?

To onboard a department is very straight forward and easy, all needed is the department student list, staff-names and department logo.

This informations help us with creating account for members of this department and also connecting account of existing memeber that already have an ordinary account with us and our AI system set up connections between all entities necessary.

What other features does THE-BRIDGE offer a department ?

When a university department onboards on our system we over them services like.

  • E-voting system with a blockchain system for their election
  • Collection of any form of payments and also generates a unique and printable receipt for payments made
  • Creation of a polling system to serve as avenue for students to make collective decisions on a topic
  • Create a controlled and effective communication channel between all memebers of the department across all board
  • Our system can connect departments of the same faculty in the same faculty in the same school together.

and so much more get in touch with us to learn more and lets onboard your department today.
